Each story is unique and demands a one-of-a-kind artistic approach. Be it a static image, or a vision in your aural world - I design sound around the narrative.

Even after two decades of playing with sound in several major motion pictures, I still feel the sense of wonderment and curiosity that I felt when I was just a kid starting out - unafraid to get creative, constantly pushing boundaries, forever evolving and aiming for a challenging, creative, versatile, technically- sound, detail-oriented design.

An intimate understanding of the narrative and its needs is one of my key strengths. I have a voracious appetite for challenges and can work well under pressure in fast-paced environments. Proficient in a variety of genres, including (but not limited to) shorts, features, ads web series, docus, gaming, virtual reality, etc. with a keen ear and senses for detail and commitment to deliver.

Skilled in collaborating with cross-functional teams, troubleshooting technical issues, committed to staying up- to-date with emerging technologies and trends.

I have the privilege of possessing a track record of success, outstanding results, and client satisfaction. And most importantly, long-lasting collaborations with creative minds.

I head WAVE WORX, where my team of home-grown artists work with me to deliver a whole new perspective on sound, and an ideal client experience.
