Each story is unique and demands a one-of-a-kind artistic approach. Balancing a deep understanding of audio technology with a keen artistic sensibility, dedicated to translating creative visions into powerful sonic realities - is exactly what I do! I design sound around the narrative.

My voracious appetite for challenges had made me adept and versatile. My diverse portfolio includes (but not limited to) shorts, features, ads, documentaries, web series, gaming, virtual reality, consultancy, etc. with a keen ear for detail and commitment to deliver.

An intimate understanding of the narrative fuelled by the curiosity to challenge traditional norms and push boundaries - is one of my key strengths. It is more than two decades now, but I have still managed to remain a novice (at heart) - unafraid to get creative, constantly pushing boundaries by experimenting with unconventional sound sources, forever evolving to enhance aural experience.

My other specialities include collaborating with cross-functional teams, troubleshooting technical issues, committed to staying up to date with emerging technologies and trends.

I have the privilege of possessing a track record of success, outstanding results, and client satisfaction. And most importantly, long-lasting collaborations with creative minds.

I head WAVE WORX, where my team of home-grown artists work with me to deliver a whole new perspective on sound, and an ideal client experience.
